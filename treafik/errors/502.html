Upstream Server Took a Coffee Break

Our gateway tried to talk to another service and got a very confused reply.

The server acting as a gateway or proxy received an invalid response from an upstream server. Translation: OctoJoom sent a perfectly polite request, and the upstream service responded with something that made absolutely no sense - or nothing at all.

Common reasons (and what you can do):
  • Temporary upstream downtime or restart - wait a moment and refresh the page.
  • Network hiccups between services - these often resolve on their own.
  • If this error is persistent, your administrator may need to check upstream server health and configuration.
